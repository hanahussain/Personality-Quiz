You got: Phlegmatic!

Overview - "Societal Supporters"

Phlegmatics are known to be relaxed and peaceful. They are and easy temperament type to get along with because they are so agreeable and patient.

Strengths

  • Well-liked
  • Easy-going
  • Hard workers

Weaknesses

  • Resist changes in their routine
  • Prone to be taken advantage of
  • Unambitious
